Focus Mode Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 20, 2024

We operate the Focus Mode mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal data when you use our Service and your choices regarding that data.

1. Information Collection and Use

Focus Mode is designed to work offline, and we do not directly collect personal data from users. However, we use Google Ads AdMob for advertising, which may collect certain information to provide personalized ads.

1.1 Advertising Data

Our app includes Google Ads AdMob services, which may collect and use data such as:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Ad interaction information

We maintain transparency in our advertising practices. You can view our authorized advertising partners and relationships in our ads.txt file.

We do not control or access the information collected by AdMob. For more information about how AdMob handles data, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

2. Use of Camera

Focus Mode uses your device's camera for face detection, a core app functionality. We do not collect, store, or transmit any images or videos from your camera. All face detection processing occurs locally on your device.

3. Offline Functionality

The core features of Focus Mode function offline, and we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data related to your use of these features.

4. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from users under 13.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page, and you are encouraged to review this Policy periodically.
